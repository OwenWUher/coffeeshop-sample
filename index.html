Our Story

More Than Coffee.
It's a Feeling.

Born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can turn any morning into something worth savoring. COFFEENAME opened its doors in 2019 with a commitment to quality beans, expert craft, and a space that feels like a second home.

We source directly from small farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships, not just supply chains. Every roast is dialed in to express the unique character of its origin.
